Mac N' Cheese
Ingrediants Directions
1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
water		 Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 8 minutes. Drain.
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
ground black pepper to taste
2 cups milk
2 cups shredded chedder cheese		 Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; stir in flour, salt, and pepper until smooth, about 5 minutes. Slowly pour milk into butter-flour mixture while continuously stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbling, about 5 minutes. Add Cheddar cheese to milk mixture and stir until cheese is melted, 2 to 4 minutes.
  Fold macaroni into cheese sauce until coated.

Beef Tacos
Ingrediants Directions
  Heat the oven (with the oven rack in the middle) to 350°.
1 yellow onion, chopped Chop the onion.
2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil Place a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the oil and heat until it shimmers (1 to 2 minutes). Add the chopped onion and cook, stirring with a wooden spoon, until softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
1 1/4 pounds ground beef
2 tablespoons chili powder
1 teaspons ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/3 cup water		 Add the beef and cook, breaking it up with your spoon, until it is no longer pink, 5 to 6 minutes. Add in the chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and salt and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add the water and cook 2 to 3 minutes more until the meat is nice and juicy but not soupy. Turn off heat.
8 corn torillas Place a small skillet over medium heat. Heat each tortilla 1 to 2 minutes per side (use tongs to flip). Keep them warm by wrapping them in foil and placing in the hot oven until you are ready.
Toppins of your choosing Get your toppings ready. Reheat the meat mixture, if necessary, then fill your tacos.

Pizza
Ingrediants Directions
1 package (1/4 ounce) active dry yeast
1 teaspoon suger
1 1/4 cups warm water (110° to 115°)
1/4 cup canola oil
1 teaspoon salt
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour		 In large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in water; let stand for 5 minutes. Add oil and salt. Stir in flour, a cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.
1/2 pound ground beef
1 small onion, chopped		 Turn onto floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 2-3 minutes. Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes. Meanwhile, cook beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink; drain.
1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
3 teaspoons dried oregano
1 teaspoon basil
1 medium green pepper, diced
2 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese		 Punch down dough; divide in half. Press each into a greased 12-in. pizza pan. Combine the tomato sauce, oregano and basil; spread over each crust. Top with beef mixture, green pepper and cheese.
  Bake at 400° for 25-30 minutes or until crust is lightly browned.

Biscuits N' Gravy
Ingrediants Directions
1 (16 ounce) can refrigerated jumbo buttermilk biscuits Bake biscuits according to package directions.
1 (9.6 ounce) package Jimmy Dean® Original Hearty Pork Sausage Crumbles
1/4 cup flour
2 1/2 cups milk
salt and ground black pepper to taste		 Meanwhile, cook sausage in large skillet over medium heat 5-6 minutes or until thoroughly heated, stirring frequently. Stir in flour. Gradually add milk; cook until mixture comes to a boil and thickens, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer 2 minutes, stirring constantly. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  Split biscuits in half. Place 2 halves on each of 8 plates; top with about 1/3 cup gravy.

Sloppy Joes
Ingrediants Directions
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 small onion, chopped
1/3 cup green pepper, chopped		 Heat oil in a large frying pan set over medium-high heat. Saute onions and green pepper for 5 minutes.
1 pound lean ground beef Add beef to the pan. Cook, stirring until browned, about 5 to 10 minutes. Drain off fat.
1 1/2 cups Heinz Tomato Ketchup
1 tablespoon Worcestershire Sauce
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
8 sandwich buns		 Stir in ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, sugar, salt and pepper. Simmer over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes or until slightly thickened. Serve on sandwich buns.
